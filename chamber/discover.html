A little bit of ourselves

At Lonitor Chamber of Commerce, our story is defined by community, innovation, and a shared commitment to sustainable economic growth. Founded by the visionary Francis Uba Ezieme—a dynamic 31-year-old who has already made significant strides in shaping economic policies—our chamber represents a new generation of business leadership. We pride ourselves on blending creative ideas with practical solutions, ensuring that every initiative is grounded in the real-world needs of entrepreneurs and local enterprises. We believe that true progress comes from collaboration. By uniting diverse voices and fostering a culture of trust and transparency, we empower businesses to thrive. Every project we undertake, every partnership we forge, and every event we host is a testament to our passion for nurturing a vibrant, resilient business community. At the heart of our mission is the commitment to uplift our members and drive positive change in the local economy—a little bit of ourselves in every effort. Join us on this journey as we continue to innovate, support, and lead the way toward a brighter commercial future for everyone in our community.
